The Best Disney Movies According to Emma

My Top 3 Disney movies of all time.

Tangled

Tangled is an animated movie that tells the story of Rapunzel, a lost young princess with magical long blonde hair. She lives in a hidden tower with her "mother," Gothel, who keeps her from leaving by telling her the world is dangerous. She meets an intruder, Flynn Rider, who helps her escape and explore the world she has never seen.

The Lion King

The Lion King is a popular animated movie produced by Disney in 1994. It tells the story of a young lion named Simba, who is destined to become the king of the Pride Lands, a kingdom of animals in Africa. However, his evil uncle Scar murders his father Mufasa and tricks Simba into thinking he is responsible. Simba runs away and grows up with two friends, Timon and Pumbaa, who teach him to live a carefree life. Years later, he meets his childhood friend Nala, who tells him that Scar has ruined the Pride Lands and that he must return to reclaim his throne. Mulan

The story is about Mulan, a teenage girl, who saved China. When there was a war in China, the Emperor announced that one man from each family should join the army. Mulan dressed herself as a man and joined the army as her father was old and her brother was a child. In the army, she proved to be a brave soldier.
